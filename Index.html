03 — ARCHITECTURAL SYSTEM

STRUCTURE & MATERIALS

Every material choice is driven by thermodynamic performance, structural logic, and long-term maintainability.

PRIMARY STRUCTURE
MODULAR HEXAFRAME
Prefabricated hexagonal steel-composite frame based on 6m × 6m × 5.2m unit cells. BFRP columns — 40% lighter than steel, thermal conductivity 0.04 W/m·K.
THERMAL ENVELOPE
ADAPTIVE SKIN
Triple-layer ETFE cushions with electrochromic membrane. Aerogel insulation: 0.015 W/m·K. PCM panels absorb 220 kJ/kg of peak thermal load.
PASSIVE COOLING
WIND & THERMAL LOGIC
City oriented at 32° from true north. Wind catchers generate 4–8 Pa pressure differentials. Thermal chimneys integrated within structural walls.

04 — URBAN PLANNING

CITY LAYOUT

Radial-nodal model — a central hub surrounded by six distinct functional sectors, each with its own microclimate management strategy.

ZONING STRATEGY
RADIAL NODAL
Central civic nucleus (Zone 0) surrounded by six sectors: Residential clusters R1–R3 (3,800 units), Research & Production Zone, and Agricultural ring — 18km of controlled-atmosphere growing structures.
HUMAN SCALE
WALKABILITY
Max walking distance to transit: 320m. No resident more than 600m from open green space. Urban density: 180 residents/hectare. 34% of floor area visually connected to planted surface.
